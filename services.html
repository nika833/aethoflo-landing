Our Approach

Strategy before the build.

The content exists. The challenge is knowing when providers are ready to receive it — and building a system that delivers it at the right point in their development. That gap is where we work.

Phase 01 · Listen

Journey mapping

Two to three weeks on the ground with supervisors, providers, and ops. We map the real journey — not the org chart version.

Phase 02 · Architect

Roadmap design

Milestones, triggers, and the skills each one demands. A roadmap your clinical leaders will recognize as theirs.

Phase 03 · Sequence

Content strategy

What gets taught at M-01 vs. M-04. Where existing modules fit, where new ones are needed, and the sequence that makes them land.

Phase 04 · Implement

aethoflo onboarding

We stand up your roadmap inside aethoflo, integrate with your provider systems, and stay embedded through the first cohort.
