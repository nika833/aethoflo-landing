Logo Explorations · 04 — Lava lamp

Six ways to flow.

Bold, wholesome, minimalist — app-icon-first. Each one leans into the lava-lamp shape language: rounded volumes, warm gradients, blobs caught mid-rise. The kind of icon that looks at home next to Chrome, OneDrive, Notion, Linear.

Why lava works for aethoflo. A lava lamp is warm, slow, continuous — and nothing inside it is ever in exactly the same position twice. That's the product in one object: learning that keeps moving, meeting the moment as it rises. The shapes are soft enough to feel wholesome (not clinical), bold enough to read at 16×16, and the warm orange–cream palette is already yours.
01 Classic two blobs rising

Warm gradient background, cream blob rising from below with a smaller droplet detached above. The most literal lava reading — and the most app-storey. Reads as energy, warmth, hospitality. Nothing to explain.

One tall organic blob rising through the frame, narrower top, fatter base. Almost a figure, almost a flame. The most serene of the set — a mark that breathes rather than pulses. Works beautifully in the nav bar at tiny sizes because it's a single silhouette.
