Logo Explorations · 02 — structural

Six fresh directions.

Geometric, kinetic, and slightly technical — each mark leans on structure rather than a circle-with-a-dot. Think signal paths, letterform architecture, and linework that could also live at 16px in a status bar.

01 Cascade

Three horizontal lines at graduating lengths — the waterfall of training bundles rolling out over time. The top tick is the signal arriving now. Reads as a waveform, an equalizer, a timeline. Techy but calm.

02 Node Path

A three-point journey — start, milestone, arrival. The middle node is filled in accent as the active moment. Reads like a graph, a route, a learning path. Distinctly product-coded without feeling like every other fintech logo.

03 Drift

Three nested chevrons — the product moving with the provider, one step ahead, one alongside, one behind. Implies direction, pace, and continuous movement. Shape-memorable; works as a favicon even at 12px.

04 Monogram AF

A custom AF monogram — the A's apex continues into the F's crossbar, one continuous line. Literary, serious, confident. Works as initial-brand in footers and print, and as a clear glyph in the app icon.

05 Isobar

Five horizontal bars at slightly varying lengths — reads as a sound waveform, a contour map, a progress ladder. The middle bar flares orange: the moment in focus. Very techy, very scannable, no wasted geometry.

06 Vector

An arrow built from a pure slash angle — the direction your training is already moving. The slash cuts through a tight grid box, so it reads as "find your flo": clear, forward, decisive. Reduces to one stroke at favicon scale.
