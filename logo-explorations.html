Logo Explorations · 01

Six concepts for aethoflo.

Each concept gets a wordmark lockup, an app-icon, and monochrome/color variations. Chosen direction should survive at 16px and feel unmistakably aethoflo across the site, the app widget, and printed materials.

01 The Moment

A signal point held between "aetho" and "flo" — the instant where training meets the moment. The dot punctuates the wordmark and becomes the app icon on its own. Most restrained, most editorial; works as pure type.
