Logo Explorations · 09 — L5 refined

The offset, held six ways.

Small circle low-left, large circle high-right — the kinetic lean you liked. Three variations with outlines (tuning weight and offset); three without outlines at all, using color alone to define the forms and the overlap.

What the outlined vs borderless split is testing. The outlined set keeps the mark graphic and icon-ready — reads clean at 16px, holds up on merch. The borderless set is softer, more editorial, more "brand" than "app icon" — it asks more of the background but rewards with a warmer, quieter presence that matches the wellness tone of the site. B3 (orange primary with a cream highlight lens) is the most unexpected; B5 (cream-on-ink) is the most premium.
